  2. Privacy

The information we collect includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, delivery address, payment details, and data about your interactions with our website, such as pages visited, links clicked, and orders placed. We also collect information about your device, including IP address, browser type, and operating system. We use this information to process and deliver your orders, respond to your inquiries, and keep you updated on the status of your orders. Additionally, if you have opted-in to receive promotional materials, we may use your information to send you news, offers, and updates about our restaurant, including new menu items, special deals, and events. We take all necessary steps to ensure the security and confidentiality of your personal data. This includes implementing robust security measures such as encryption, secure servers, and strict access controls. Our staff is trained to handle your information with care, and we require any third-party service providers to adhere to similar standards. We also have measures in place to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. We regularly review and update our security procedures to ensure they remain effective and up-to-date. You have certain rights regarding your personal data. These include the right to request access to your data, corrections to your data, and deletion of your data. You also have the right to opt-out of receiving promotional materials from us. If you have any questions or concerns about our privacy practices or would like to exercise any of these rights, please don't hesitate to contact us. We recognize that our privacy policy may change from time to time. When we make changes, we'll update this policy to reflect the new practices. By continuing to interact with our website or online ordering system, you will be bound by the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we're protecting your information. Our commitment to your privacy is ongoing, and we're dedicated to maintaining the trust you've placed in us. If you have any concerns or questions, we're here to listen and assist. In addition to the above, we may also use your information to: Improve our website and online ordering system Develop new services and features Respond to feedback and suggestions Enhance your overall experience with us We're committed to being transparent about our data practices and to protecting your personal information. We're confident that our measures will ensure the security and confidentiality of your data. If you have any further questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out. We're here to help.
